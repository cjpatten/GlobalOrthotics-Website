How It Works

From Scan to Finished Device

Our digital workflow streamlines the path from clinical prescription to finished device — with consistency at every step.

Step 01

Scan

Using the iPad's built-in camera or an attached Structure Sensor, precise foot geometry is captured in seconds — no plaster, no mess.

Step 02

Design

The digital prescription defines every parameter — arch geometry, heel cup depth, posting angles — with no interpretation required.

Step 03

Manufacture

Industrial 3D printing builds the shell layer by layer, using only the material the device requires — precise, consistent, zero waste.

Step 04

Deliver

3D printing means no backlog and no bottlenecks — quality-verified devices shipped to your practice with consistently fast turnaround times.

Why Both Methods Matter

The right tool for every patient

Sub-millimeter Accuracy with 3D Printing

Our 3D printing capability builds orthotic shells layer by layer with sub-millimeter precision. For cases where digital reproducibility and zonal stiffness control matter most, it's a powerful addition to our traditional manufacturing expertise.

Digital Reproducibility

With 3D printed devices, the original digital file is stored permanently. Replacements and duplicates are exact reprints — whether it's six months or six years later. For traditional devices, our experienced technicians deliver the same consistency they've been trusted for over 25 years.

Traditional Craftsmanship, Proven Results

Our traditional manufacturing methods have served practitioners for over 25 years — and they remain a core part of what we offer. Different patients and clinical needs call for different approaches. The best labs give you the choice, not just one option.

Quality-First, Not First-to-Market

We spent over a year evaluating technology, testing workflows, and perfecting our design recipe before offering 3D printed orthotics. We waited until we could do it right — the same standard our practitioners have always expected from us.

About Us

25 Years of Excellence, Now Expanded

Global Orthotics Inc. is a Canadian orthotic lab built on one principle: the device should match the patient's needs exactly. For over 25 years, our team has delivered that standard through traditional manufacturing. Now, we've added precision 3D printing — not as a replacement, but as a powerful expansion of what we can offer.

This is not a pivot. It is an evolution. We spent over a year investigating technology, testing workflows, and perfecting a design recipe before bringing 3D printing to our practitioners. The same quality-first approach that built our reputation is exactly what guided this expansion.

  • Traditional and 3D printed orthotics — the right method for every patient
  • 3D scanning, foam impression, and plaster casting all accepted
  • App-based ordering — scan, prescribe, and track from one place
  • A Canadian lab with the personal service practitioners trust

Our Products

Built for the Clinical Outcome

Different patients need different devices — and different manufacturing methods. We offer both traditional and 3D printed options so you can choose the right approach for every case.

Dress Orthotics

Slim footwear demands a precise, low-profile shell. Available in both traditional and 3D printed options, our dress orthotics are built to fit the shoe they were designed for — reliably and comfortably.

  • Vinyl top cover
  • 2mm functional shell
  • Multiple shell materials

Sports Orthotics

Performance patients demand results they can feel on the move. Built to correct imbalance, restore function, and support active lifestyles — with the durability and precision your athletes expect.

  • Mesh / ETC top cover
  • Poron midlayer
  • Extrinsic rearfoot post

Diabetic Orthotics

For diabetic patients, pressure distribution and fit are clinical priorities. Our accommodative options — now available in 3D printed construction — deliver the precision and consistency these patients require.

  • Plastazote top cover
  • 1mm soft shell
  • Pressure-relieving arch fill

EVA Orthotics

Ethylene Vinyl Acetate construction designed to cushion and offload pressure points. A comfortable, precisely built device that works across the patient's everyday footwear — improving daily wear rate and clinical outcomes.

  • EVA foam construction
  • Cushioning support
  • Pressure offloading

Paediatric Orthotics

With children, the timing and accuracy of foot correction directly affects development. Our paediatric orthotics are built with the care and precision that growing feet demand — in both traditional and 3D printed options.

  • Alignment correction
  • Growth-friendly design
  • Activity support

Casual Orthotics

An orthotic that fits more shoes gets worn more often. Slim and precisely built for walking, hiking, and everyday use — designed to move between footwear without compromising comfort or correction.

  • Slim profile
  • All-day comfort
  • Lace-up compatible

Prescriptive 3DP Orthotic

Precision engineered and clinically proven. Repeatable accuracy eliminates technician variability, zonal stiffness control lets you design around each patient's specific needs, and 3D printed shells are stronger without added bulk.

  • 3D printed nylon shell
  • Zonal stiffness control
  • 2–3 day turnaround

Accommodative 3DP Orthotic

The orthotic you always wanted to offer — now you can. 3D printing makes accommodative devices practical to produce at scale, with the same digital precision. Ideal for diabetic, elderly, and post-surgical patients.

  • Soft pressure-relieving design
  • Digital reproducibility
  • Complex patient profiles

Technology & Manufacturing

The Tools, Products, and Processes Behind Every Device

A fully integrated ecosystem — from a modern scan-and-order app to industrial 3D printing — built to give practitioners more control and patients better outcomes.

Global Scan & Order App

A mobile-first app that covers the full workflow — capture patient pathology, scan feet using the iPad's built-in camera or an attached Structure Sensor, configure prescriptions with intuitive touch controls, submit orders, track status, and get support. Works for both traditional and 3D printed orthotic orders.

3D Scanning Prescription Design Order Tracking Traditional & 3D

3D Printed Custom Orthotics

Two new 3D printed products — the Prescriptive Functional orthotic for precision correction with zonal stiffness control, and the Accommodative orthotic for diabetic, elderly, and post-surgical patients. Both built with sub-millimeter accuracy and full digital reproducibility.

Prescriptive Functional Accommodative Zonal Stiffness Digital Reproducibility

100% Digital Workflow

From scan to finished device, the entire production chain operates digitally. No interpretation, no manual transcription, no variability between shifts. Digital files mean exact replacements months or years later, predictable turnaround, and scalable production.

End-to-End Digital Predictable Turnaround Exact Replacements Scalable

Additive Manufacturing

Industrial 3D printing using proven platforms — EOS, HP Multi Jet Fusion, and Mosaic technologies. PA12 Nylon powder for strong, slim functional shells. FFF TPU filament for soft, pressure-relieving accommodative devices. The right material and process for every clinical need.
